Windfall Island

Windfall is one of many islands populating the Great Sea. Located in the northwest, this island has become a popular hub for trade and commerce.

One of the most heavily populated islands in the sea, Windfall is a lively and joyful place year-round.

History

Windfall Island was founded after the Great Flood destroyed the ancient kingdom of Hyrule. Citizens from Kakariko Village and Hyrule Castle Town

relocated to the mountains when their homes were swept away. There, they founded the township of Windfall, which grew to be a hub of activity much like

its Hyrulean predecessors. The notable windmill in the center of town is a holdover from old Kakariko, a reminder of Windfall's storied history.
